About Me

Curious by nature,
driven by craft.

I'm Melody — a social media marketer and designer who's never been afraid to try something new. My path hasn't been a straight line, and that's exactly what makes me unique.

I've shown dedication across several different fields — from working with kids to retail and service — and I bring that same commitment to everything I take on. Right now, I channel it into designing and creating content for a health brand, work I find both meaningful and creatively rewarding.

That breadth of experience gives me a genuine understanding of people that shapes how I approach every piece of content I make. I show up curious, adaptable, and ready to put in the work.
